FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Procedure?
  1. Client Application: The process begins when the client fills and submits the application form above.
  2. NHD Form: Upon submission the client recieves the link to get the NHD Forms ($19) to fill and complete thier application.
  3. Job Contract: The Agency secures a Job for the applicant in one of it partner companies within 1 month and sends the contract to the client for signature.
  4. Visa Sponsorship: The agency secures a fully sponsored visa for the client.
  5. Travel Arrangements and Bills: The agency handles all travel arrangements and Bills, including flights tickets.
  6. Arrival: Upon arrival, the client is recieved at the Airport and droven to thier accomodation. The client is given 2 weeks to rest and addapt then resume work the following week

No Extra cost! The only payment you will make is for the NHD forms. That is $19 for individal sponsorship.
